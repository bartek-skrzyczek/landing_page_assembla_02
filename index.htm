Why Assembla?

Built from the ground up, Assembla was designed to meet the needs of both project managers and developers. It's one place to manage teams, projects and code.

Collaboration made easy

Tired of jumping from a generic repo tool to a generic project management tool? With Assembla, you can do everything in one platform from creating tickets to adding repos to developing wikis. Add your designs, diagrams and all media assets directly to the app and enjoy unlimited storage. Collaboration has never been easier. Learn More.
 
 

Tools just for developers

With unlimited repos, you can choose Git, SVN and Perforce. Integrate your respository directly into your workflow seamlessly. Whether you're conducting code reviews, getting input on story definition iterations or managing collaborative development of push/pull to a shared staging environment to show off the latest work to the rest of the team - you can do all of this with Assembla. Learn More.

Task Management

Customize ticket statuses and milestones to speak the language of your team. Use cardwall view to easily drag and drop tickets into different statuses. With real-time reporting and visibility our analytics and real-time views can guide your team and adjust work on the fly. There's no limit to the amount of customization you can do in the app. Learn More.
